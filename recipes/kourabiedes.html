Kourabiedes

Description

Kourabiedes, though originally from Turkey, are regardless an amazing Greek dessert. Some overly proud Greeks may scoff at praise of an originally Turkish dessert, or deny their Turkish origins entirely, but this author pays those sentiments little mind. The tastebuds care little for the little cookies' origins when they are brought to life by buttery and almondy deliciousness.

This receipe is fairly straightforward: make dough with lots of butter and crushed almonds, bake, and cover in powdered sugar. I could go in to more detail, perhaps at a later time.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Butter
  • Almonds
  • Other Stuff

Steps

  1. Place almonds in a bag, and crush via rolling pin.
  2. Combine butter, sugar, crushed almonds, and other stuff in a bowl.
  3. Mold in to desired shape and bake.
  4. Once done, cover in powedered sugar.
  5. Profit.
