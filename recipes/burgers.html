Burgers

Description

Burgers

'Nuff said.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Buns
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Roll beef in to 1 ounce balls.
  2. Place on to hot cast iron pan.
  3. Smash flat on pan.
  4. Cook for one minute and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Scrape burger off pan and flip. Cook for 30 more seconds
  6. Optional Add cheese.
